 Munnar
Indian hill town with Nallathanni tea Museum, waterfalls & Eravikulam National Park.

Temples, Sculpture, Tigers and Safaris.




Kolkata
West Bengal capital home to Mother Teresa's tomb, British-colonial architecture & art galleries.


 Shimla
Himachal Pradesh capital known for colonial architecture, Himalayan views & shopping on The Mall.

 Darjeeling
Indian hill town known for tea plantations, Himalayan views & "Toy Train" narrow gauge railway.

 Fatehpur Sikri
Indian city with red sandstone Mughal architecture, from Jama Masjid mosque to Salim Chisti's tomb.




Chennai

 Bengaluru
India tech hub known for parks, nightlife, Bangalore Palace, & Lalbagh Botanical Garden.


 Hampi
Ancient Indian village dotted with Vijayanagara ruins & known for Virupaksha Temple & Hampi Bazaar.

 Andaman and Nicobar Islands
